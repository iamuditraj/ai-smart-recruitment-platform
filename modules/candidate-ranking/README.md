# 🏆 Candidate Ranking Module

This module defines the logic used to rank and categorize candidates after their resumes have been screened by the Weighted ATS Engine.

## 🚀 Overview

The Ranking Module takes the raw numerical scores generated by the ATS and maps them to actionable statuses. This allows recruiters to quickly filter and identify the most promising talent at a glance.

### How it Works

1.  **Input**: A numerical ATS score (0-100) and candidate profile data.
2.  **Processing**: The engine evaluates the score against predefined thresholds.
3.  **Output**: A categorized status and a UI-ready "badge class".

---

## 📊 Ranking Logic

Candidates are categorized into three primary tiers:

| Score Range | Status | Badge Class | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| **80 – 100** | `Shortlist` | `badge-success` | Top-tier match; recommended for immediate interview. |
| **60 – 79** | `Review` | `badge-warning` | Solid match; requires manual recruiter review. |
| **0 – 59** | `Reject` | `badge-danger` | Does not meet core requirements or lacks critical experience. |

### Sorting & Display

In the Recruiter Dashboard, candidates are automatically sorted by their **ATS Match Score** in descending order. This ensures that the most relevant profiles appear at the top of the interface.
